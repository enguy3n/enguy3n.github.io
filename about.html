My name is Ethan Nguyen, I graduated from the University of Computer Science in May of 2025 with a degree in Computer Science and a Minor in Creative Technology and Design. As someone passionate about people, I found one of my favorite parts of programming was building things that the average person could understand. In school, I focused on building appealing websites, both from a UI/UX perspective and a programming one. On my own, my drive to focus on people also lead me to jobs that focused on tech support, tech teaching, and helping others. Currently, I'm working on my CompTIA A+ certification through Goodwill's LIFT program and working as an intern technician at Think Green Computers in Colorado Springs.

My computer science work, both academically and extracurricularly, includes creating terminal programs for tasks ranging from basic computation to accessing APIs for data processing. I also have created web-based applications such as a rudimentary pixel art creator and a star movement simulator using tools such as Javascript, NodeJS, and Flask. I love the process of creating applications, from designing for aesthetics and functionality, to programming finalized products. I've done minor experimentation in data science and data processing, as well as working with microcontrollers for electrical/mechanical systems. As a former freelance artist, I have extensive experience doing customer support/service on clients and working with them iteratively on a project to achieve optimal results.

In my freetime, I like to spend time hiking, learning to cook/bake, improving my illustrative skills, and taking care of my assortment of freshwater invertebrates. I love to learn and expand my skills, and believe that doing this not only makes me stronger in what I am learning, but also in finding new knowledge to apply in other fields.
