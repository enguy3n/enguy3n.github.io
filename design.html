Colorado Symphony Classics

Tools:
Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Indesign
Completion Date:
04/10/23

A mockup flyer created for ATLS2300: Text, a class at CU Boulder. The assignment was to create a flyer that included text provided by the Colorado Symphony for their 2023/24 Classics season. The assignment was limited: you could only use four or less Pantone colors, had to make the poster a certain size, and were only allowed to use graphics if they were symbols, so the goal was to make a visually appealing poster (focusing on typography) while including all required text.
