FREYJA SIMULATOR

FREYJA SIMULATOR is an adapted retelling of Hyndluliod (The Song of Hyndla). Also known as Little Völuspá, Hyndluliod tells the story of Freyja travelling to the giantess Hyndla.

DISCLAIMER: Freyja Simulator is not an accurate retelling of Hyndluliod. Also, I didn't code this well, so this will break if you pick a new options without waiting for text to finish crawling, sorry.
